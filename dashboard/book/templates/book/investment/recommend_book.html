  • CAGR
    • 연복리 수익률
    • Compound Annual Growth Rate
    • \(CAGR=\left(\frac{EB}{BB}\right)^{(\frac{1}{n})}\)-1
    • \(EB\): Ending balance
    • \(BB\): Beginning balance
    • \(n\): Number of years
  • MDD
    • 최대 낙폭
    • Maximum Drawdown
    • \(MDD=\frac{P-L}{P}\)
    • \(P\): Peak value before largest drop
    • \(L\): Lowest value before new high established
